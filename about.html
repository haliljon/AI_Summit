APPLYING AI & MACHINE LEARNING TO FINANCE & TECHNOLOGY 2023

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics and activists who comprise the Scaling Artificial Intelligence affiliates network in a different country every four years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge. So, if you're active and engaged on the worlds open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.
